将来完成时

结构

主 + shall/ will + have + 过去分词

时间状语

  • before + 将来时间

  • by + 将来时间

  • by the end for + 将来时间

  • eg. I will have finished a novel by tomorrow evening.

定义

(1. 表示在将来某一时间以前己经完成或一直持续的动作

将来完成时用来表示在将来某一时间以前己经完成或一直持续的动作

  • eg. We shall have learned 12 units by the end of this term.

    到这个学期末，我们将学完12个单元。

  • eg. By the time you get home I will have cleaned the house from top to bottom.

    你到家之前我将把房子彻底打扫一遍。

  • eg. The train will have left before we get to the station.

    在我们到之前火车已经离开了（还没到）。

(2. 表示推测

表示推测，相当于"must have done"结构。

  • eg. You will have heard of this, I guess.

    我想你已经听说过这件事了。

  • eg. I am sure he will have got the information.

    我相信他一定得到了这个信息。

(3. 表示“可能性” ，或“设想”

  • eg. It's five o'clock， they will have arrived home by now.

    已经五点钟了，他们现在应该已经到家了。

  • eg. As you will have noticed, there is a new secretary in the front office.

    也许你们已经注意到了，前面的办公室来了一位新秘书。

(4. 现在完成时可以代替将来完成时

在时间从句和条件从句中，现在完成时可以代替将来完 成时，表示将来某时已完成的动作。

  • eg. You'll get to like the subject after you have studied it for some time.

    在学习这个学科一段时间之后，你就会喜欢它的。

  • eg. I'll go and see the exhibition as soon as I have finished my work.

    我一做完作业就去看展览。

练习

  • be in 待在某地(状态)

课文

The Olympic Games will be held in our country in four years' time.

  • in four years’ time 四年之后

    • in常与将来时连用表示“……时间之后”

    • eg. He will be back in ten minutes or half an hour.

      他将在十分钟或半小时后回来

  • four years later 四年后

  • four years ago 四年前

As a great many people will be visiting the country, the government will be building new hotels, an immense stadium, and a new Olympic-standard swimming pool.

  • a great many people = a large number of people

They will also be building new roads and a special railway line.

  • a special railway line 专线铁路

Workers will have completed the new roads by the end of this year.

  • by the end of this year 到今年年底前
  • by 是完成时的标志，表示“到…为止， 在…之前” 不能与表示一段时间的名词连用，只能与表示时间点的名词或词组连用

Everybody will be watching anxiously as the new buildings *go

We are all very excited and are looking forward to the Olympic Games because they have never been held before in this country.

出门测

  • look forward to (doing) sth. 期望做某事

  • 被动语态：be + V.过分

  • because + 句子 ， because of + 名词/名词短语 = due to

  • few 修饰可数名词， little 修饰不可数名词

    很少；几乎没有(否定) 有一些；有一点(肯定)
    可数名词复数 few a few
    不可数名词 little a little
